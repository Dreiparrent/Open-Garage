About

Welcome to the wonderful world of Open Garage. What makes it wonderful is it’s your world – your communities. You’ve made the decision to live where you live, work where you work, and study where you go to school. Maybe you’re new, and maybe you’re grinding until you make Partner. Either way, chances are you aren’t very familiar with the people who surround you on a daily basis. Our goal is to change that – to expose all of the untapped knowledge and talent that surrounds us, so that we can help out each other, build real relationships, and ultimately build better communities.

Why “Open Garage”? Think about your garage at home, or your parents, or your friends. What’s in there? A car, sure. But what is it that’s hanging from the rafters, built into the wall, and piled in the corner? It’s your gear, equipment, toys, and tools; it’s what you play with, what you tinker with, and what you fix things with. It’s everything that enables you to pursue your passions and exercise your skills. Let’s open that up and show our communities what we have to offer.

Values

Connect

Open Garage was born out of a desire to connect people offline. As a result of living in such a perpetually connected world, we have become some of the most disconnected people to the world around us.

Contribute

The premise of Open Garage is that everyone, BECAUSE of their race, creed, ethnicity, sexual orientation, gender, religion, hair style, or music choice, has something to contribute to the greater good.

Respect

We welcome everyone to the Open Garage community. We don’t require you to share the same beliefs and values as your community members, but we do require you to respect them.
